Century No.86 
Score: 138 
Series: 2009 tri-series in Sri Lanka featuring India, Sri Lanka and New Zealand 
Match: Final: India V Sri Lanka 
Venue: Colombo (Premadasa) 
Result: India won by 46 runs A year after he guided India to a title triumph in Australia, Sachin took India to another title win. This time, it was in Sri Lanka in the final of tri-series. Batting first after winning the toss, India put up 319 on the board thanks to 138 by Sachin. The Little Master's knock was laced with 10 fours and 1 six. The Lankans fought hard but folded up 46 runs short.
