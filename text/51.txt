Century No.51 
Score: 146 
Series: Bilateral ODI series, 2000 
Match: India V Zimbabwe, 3rd ODI 
Venue: Jodhpur 
Result: Zimbabwe won by 1 wicket The brilliant 146 by the Little Master was filled with 17 hits to the fence and propelled India to 284 in their 50 overs. This was later overshadowed by a magnificent partnership between the Flower brothers for Zimbabwe, who chased down the target with 1 ball to spare and 1 wicket in hand.
