Result: Match drawn After registering one of their finest triumphs, India came to Cape Town with a chance to win a series on South African soil for the first time ever. Given a tough target of 340 runs to win their maiden series on South African soil, India decided not to take the risk of losing the match. The two teams settled for a draw.
