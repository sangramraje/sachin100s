Century No.90 
Score: 143 
Series: Bilateral test series, 2010 
Match: India V Bangladesh, 2nd test 
Venue: Mirpur 
Result: India won by 10 wickets Days after scoring a wonderful century to help India to respectability at Chittagong, Sachin came up with the goods again at Mirpur. He blasted 143 off just 182 balls as India made 544 in their first innings and that was enough to take them to a sound win in the match and wrap up the series 2-0.
