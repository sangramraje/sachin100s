Century No.98 
Score: 120 
Series: World Cup 2011 
Match: India V England, Group B 
Venue: Bangalore 
Result: Match tied It was a world cup classic. Led by Sachin's superb 120, India made a mammoth 338, which was laced with 5 towering sixes in a relatively small Chinnaswamy stadium. Strauss and Zaheer Khan later stole the show with the bat and the ball as the match ended in an absorbing tie.
