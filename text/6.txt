Score: 104* 
Match: India Vs Sri Lanka 
Series: India in Sri Lanka 1993, 2nd Test 
Venue: Sinhalese Sports Club Ground, Colombo 
Result: India won by 235 runs An unbeaten century in the second innings helped India set a massive 472-run target against the Lankans. India won the Test handsomely and went on to draw the final game, thereby winning the series 1-0.
